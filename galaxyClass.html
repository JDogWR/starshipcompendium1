The Enterprise-D is the ship from the very popular Star Trek: The Next Generation television series. It is a Galaxy-Class starship with over 1,000 people on board. It was the first time that Starfleet experimented with putting families on board starships. It was similar to a luxury cruise liner, but it also had the most advanced systems that Starfleet had to offer. From labs to weapons to swimming pools, this ship truly had it all. It even included the new holodecks.
